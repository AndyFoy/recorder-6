Mapping- Zoom In

To zoom in on a section of a map:

  • Select the zoom tool:

    • Select Map – Zoom from the Main menu.

    • Click the button on the active toolbar.

    • Press the short-cut key <F2>.

  • The map cursor will change to .

    • Click on the map, and the size of the map will be doubled. It will be redrawn centred on the point at which you clicked.

    • Alternatively, click and hold down the primary mouse button and then drag the mouse. This will draw a rectangle on the map. Draw a rectangle around the area you want to enlarge. When you release the mouse button, the map will be redisplayed, so that the rectangle you drew is as large as possible within the screen area.

Alternatively, you can use the Map navigator tool to zoom to the site of a particular polygon or piece of background

Survey Events

Survey events describe visits by surveyors to localities. A survey is composed of a set of such visits, and samples are made during these visits. The information recorded for a survey event details where surveyors went, who took part and the date (or date range). Information on conditions during the visit, and a comment to allow amplification (for example, to give extra details of exactly where surveyors went) can also be recorded.

For ad hoc observations, the survey event will contain the 'where', 'who' and 'when' component of a record.

The locality can be an entry in the Locations window, but this is not required. Alternatively a grid reference could be given, preferably with some amplification in the Location name field (e.g. TL291341, 2km east of Eden beside the A99).

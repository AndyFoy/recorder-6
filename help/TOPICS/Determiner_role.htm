Determiner role term list

The determiner role is used on taxon and biotope determinations where the required value is selected from the determiner role term list - illustrated below. If the required role is not present users can add it as specified in Adding a term to a list.

Adding, editing and deleting determiner role term list items is the same as for most others term list items - see Adding a term to a list - except for the Additional Information:

  • Validation Competency

    • 1 -  Competent naturalist.

    • 2 -  'County recorder' or equivalent.

    • 3 -  'National recorder' or equivalent.

  • Hide - tick this box if you want to block the use of this item for data entry, e.g. if you do not want it to appear on the drop down list for Role on taxon and biotope determinations.

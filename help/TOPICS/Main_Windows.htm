Recorder's main windows

The main windows in Recorder all have a similar format.

Click on the buttons and different parts of the window to find out more.

In the hierarchy on the left:

  • Click the button immediately to the left of an item to expand it and show the items nested below it at the next lower level of the hierarchy. The button will become a , and clicking this will hide the nested items again. Double-clicking on the text of an item has the same effect as clicking these buttons.

NOTE: Even if an item does not have any items nested below it, a button will still be shown until it is first clicked (except in the Taxon, Biotope and Administrative areas dictionary browsers). Once it has been clicked, the button will disappear unless there are nested items to display.

  • Select an item by clicking on the text. It will be highlighted to indicate that it is the currently selected item. Details about this item will be shown in the panel on the right.

  • You can also move the highlight, indicating the selected item, up and down the tree using the <up arrow>, <down arrow>, <PgUp>, <PgDn>, <Home> and <End> keys.

  • Right click to see a menu of actions you can perform.

In the detail panel on the right:

  • There are often many detailed fields relating to a particular item, and these are grouped in a series of tabbed pages (Page tabs).

  • The top-most, 'General' tab shows a summary of the most frequently used information.

  • Click on a tab to display different pages of information.

Features Damage Occurrences Tab

This page is used to record a particular incidence of damage.

 

Date

Required, vague date.

The date on which the damage occurred, or was noticed.

Comment

Required, RTF Memo.

Text describing the damage.

 

To add a damage occurrence record:

  • Click on the button, and the detail panel will go into Edit mode with all the fields empty.

  • Type in the required information.

    • Click the button to save your changes.

    • Click the button to abandon your changes.

To delete a damage occurrence record:

  • Highlight the damage occurrence record that you want to delete.

  • Click the button.

  • You will be asked to confirm that you really want to delete this record.

To edit a damage occurrence record aim:

  • Highlight the damage occurrence record that you want to edit.

  • Click the button to put the detail panel into Edit mode.

  • Make the changes you require.

    • Click the button to save your changes.

    • Click the button to abandon your changes.

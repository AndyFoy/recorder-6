Display unchecked observations

To display unchecked, zero-abundance or failed/pending verification observations:

  • Select Data Entry - Display Unchecked records from the Main menu.

  • Select the type of occurrences you wish to display by ticking the appropriate options.

  • Click [OK]. The Observation window will open with a filter applied so that only observations of the required type are displayed.

Tip: When the observation window opens, you will only see Surveys listed. Click a survey and press * on the numeric keypad to expand the survey and show all the selected observations within it.

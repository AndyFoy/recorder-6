Confidential observations

Individual observations can be marked as Confidential.

If an observation is flagged as Confidential:

  • It cannot be viewed by users with an access level below 'Full-edit'

  • It cannot be exported by anyone unless a systems manager has ticked the Enable export of confidential occurrences option on the General tab in Tools - Options.

  • It will not appear in reports unless you specifically indicate that confidential observations should be included (Reports – Wizard – Additional filters – Constraints). This option is only available to those users with 'full-edit' access or above.

The Confidential flag can be used to protect 'sensitive' observations where it is thought that environmental damage might occur if the information became more widely known.

It might also be used to make sure that work in progress is not accidentally reported or exported. For example, if a survey is undertaken under contract, it may be specified that the information is not released until the work is completed and the contractor's permission has been given.

Confidential observations can only be exported by users with full edit privileges.

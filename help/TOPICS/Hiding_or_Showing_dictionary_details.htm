Showing or hiding dictionary details

The three dictionary browsers can show details about the selected item in the right-hand pane of the window. You can choose whether or not you want these details displayed. Turning off the display of details makes it quicker to move around within the dictionary, but you may be more likely choose an incorrect item.

To hide the details:

  • Select Edit - Hide Item Details from the menus.

  • Click the Hide items details button in the active toolbar.

To show the details:

  • Select Edit - Show Item Details from the menus.

  • Click the Hide items details button in the active toolbar.

If the detail pane is visible, the commands to hide them are available. Once they are hidden, the menu option and toolbar buttons change to those to show them again.

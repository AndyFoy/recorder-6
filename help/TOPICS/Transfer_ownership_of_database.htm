Transfer ownership of an entire database

Warning:This process will stop you editing your own records-The only person with the right to edit those records will be the copy of the programme that you have transferred ownership to. To transfer ownership of the whole database;

  • Open Recorder

  • Select Tools-Database Tools-Reassign Database to new custodian

  • Enter your current site ID and the SITE ID of the new owner in the appropriate boxes.

  • Click on [OK]

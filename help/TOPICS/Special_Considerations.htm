Importing documents - special considerations

Most biological recording packages do not segregate the details about documentary references into separate fields (title, journal, volume, pages, etc.), but store the whole citation in a single memo field. It is extremely difficult to automate the process of splitting up such a memo into its constituent parts. References vary greatly, depending on the type of publication, and the particular style adopted for citing references by different organisations and individuals.

NOTE: If documentary references are imported from other software into this format, the 'Full reference' will appear in the read-only memo field in the General page, but the fields in the Details page will not be filled.

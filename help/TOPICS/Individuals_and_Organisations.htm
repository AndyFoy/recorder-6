Individuals and Organisations

Individuals and organisations are treated separately. Their details are stored in different tables in the underlying database, and there are some differences in detail in the information that is managed about them. For example, individuals have forenames and a surname, whereas organisations have a name and, optionally, an acronym. However, much information (addresses, contact numbers, etc.) is common to both, and is therefore managed in the one window.

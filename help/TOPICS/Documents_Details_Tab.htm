Documents Details Tab

The fields you see in this page depend upon the type of publication selected in the General page. Only those fields that are relevant for the type of publication will appear, and some labels change (for example, 'Place of Publication' for a book becomes 'Awarding Institution' for a thesis).

 

Title

Required.

RTF memo. The title of the publication.

Journal

Selection from a term-list. Journal names are maintained in a term list. You can add to this list using Tools – Term lists.

Volume

Free text. The volume number of a journal, book etc.

Part

Free text. The part number.

Number

Free text. The number for newsletters and similar publications.

Pages

Free text. The page number on which an article appears, or the number of pages in a book. You can enter a range here (e.g. 31-37) for an article in a journal or symposium.

Suppl.

Free text. Supplement details.

Edition

Free text. Edition details.

Symposium title

Free text. The title of the symposium, if this is a symposium volume or a book containing articles by many authors.

Editor(s)

The names of one or more editors of the symposium or book in which an article was published.

Publisher

Free text. Name of the publisher.

Place of publication

Free text. Place where the publisher is based or the awarding institution for a thesis.

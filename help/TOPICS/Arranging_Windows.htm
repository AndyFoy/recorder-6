Arranging windows

When a particular window is opened for the first time within Recorder, the screen display size will be dependent upon its resolution, and the overall size of the application's window. Consequently, not all the controls may be visible.

You can re-size the window by dragging its edges or corners with the mouse.

However, you may find that the relative widths of the hierarchy on the left and the detail panel on the right are not as required. To correct this, drag the raised double lines in the bar between the two panels with your mouse.

Adjust the size of the window and the position of the bar between the two panels until it appears as required. When you close the application, these settings are remembered, and next time you use the application, the window should appear as you left it.

NOTE: Maximising the window (by clicking the maximise button in the window title), or selecting Window – Cascade from the Main menu will alter the way the window is displayed. If you then close the application, these new settings will be saved.

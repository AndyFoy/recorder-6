Report Wizard Remove Items

To remove items from the selected list:

  • Highlight an item in the 'Selected' list by clicking on it with your mouse. Once an item in the list is selected, you can move the highlight up and down the list with the <up arrow>, <down arrow>, <PgUp>, <PgDn>, <Home> or <End> keys.

  • Choose the button to remove the selected item from the 'Selected list'.

OR

  • Double click an item in the 'Selected' list, and it will be removed.

OR

  • Choose the button to remove all items from the 'Selected' list in one go.

Map Options - Polygons

This facility allows you to:

  • Choose the colour for selected and unselected polygons

  • Turn the display of polygons on or off. This can be useful if you want to print a distribution map without showing polygons.

To choose the colours for polygons:

Click on the colour selection buttons for selected or unselected polygons and choose a new colour from the Colour Selection dialog. The drawing to the right shows what a selected and an unselected polygon will look like in the new colour.

NOTE: changes to the colours of polygons on the map are not made until you press the "Close" button on the Map Options dialog. If you have many polygons on the map, redrawing them in a new, unselected colour may take some time. A progress bar is shown.

To turn the display of polygons on or off:

Click on the tick-box labelled "Polygon layer visible". Polygons will be displayed if there is a tick shown in this tick-box.

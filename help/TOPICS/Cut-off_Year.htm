Cut-off year for distribution map symbols

This is the year at which 10km, 5km, 2km or 1km symbols on distribution maps change from hollow to filled-in circles. Observations dated before this year will be shown as hollow symbols, those dated during or after this year will be filled in.

  • The cut-off year is set in the Map Options - Distribution symbols window.

  • Enter a four-digit year number between 1900 and the current year.

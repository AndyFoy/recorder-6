Rich Text Format Memos

Rich Text Format (RTF) memos allow paragraphs of text to be entered which include text enhancements such as italicised, emboldened or underlined words and phrases. They will also accept accented characters, other special characters and embedded paragraph breaks.

To embolden, italicise or underline text:

  • Select the text you wish to enhance.

    • Edit – bold, italic or underline from the Main menu.
    • Press the , , or buttons from the active toolbar.
    • Press the short cut keys <Ctrl-B>, <Ctrl-I> or <Ctrl-U>.

  • Alternatively, whilst you are typing, use one of these methods to turn on emboldening, italicising or underlining. The text you type will then be enhanced, until you turn enhancement off again.

To insert special characters not available on your keyboard:

  • Use the Windows character map to copy the required character to the clipboard, and then paste it into the text.

  • Hold down the <Alt> key and type the ASCII decimal character number of the character on the numeric keypad (e.g. [ALT 130] produces é).

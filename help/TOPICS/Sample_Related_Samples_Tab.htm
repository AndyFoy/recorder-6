Sample Related Samples Tab

This page allows related samples to be grouped. For example, a series of samples may have been taken along a transect or at a different dates from the same sampling stations. In some later analysis, you may wish to consider these samples as a group.

To relate another sample to the currently selected one:

  • Ensure the detail page is in Edit mode (the [Save] and [Cancel] buttons will be active).

    • Drag the sample into the grid.

    • Or, choose the button. The Find sample dialog will open. Select the required sample, and choose [OK] or press the <Enter> key.

To remove a related sample:

  • Highlight the sample you wish to remove from the list.

  • Choose the button.

  • You will be asked to confirm that you really want to delete the item.

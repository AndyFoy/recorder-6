Changing your password

Accessed by Tools – Change Password from the Main menu.

  • Type your existing password at the 'Old Password' prompt, although if your password is still "password" this can be left blank.

  • Type your new password at the 'New Password' prompt. It must be at least 3 characters, and can consist of any combination of letters and digits. You will only see a string of "*" on the screen.

  • Type your new password again in the 'Confirm New Password' prompt. You will not be allowed to proceed unless the text matches your 'New Password'. This protects you against minor typing errors, which could result in setting an incorrect password and finding you cannot log on to the application next time!

    • Choose [OK] to actually change your password.

    • Choose [Cancel] if you decide not to proceed with the change. Your password will not be changed.

Note: Passwords are case sensitive. If you set your password to "Fred", then "fred" or "FRED" will NOT be accepted.

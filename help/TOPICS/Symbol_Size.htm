Symbol size for distribution maps

This is selected by:

  • Selecting Map – Distribution points from the Main menu, and then selecting the desired size from the sub-menu.

  • Clicking the button on the active toolbar, and selecting the desired size from the menu which appears below the button.

The options are:

  • 10km

  • 5km
  • 2km
  • 1km
  • 100m
  • Default

The km squares show distribution as presence in grid squares of the size selected. The symbol will consist of a coloured circle which may be filled or hollow, depending upon the date of the survey event, sample or observation. They will be filled if the date is during or after the year specified in the Map Options - Distribution Symbols screen – otherwise they will be hollow.

Distributions are initially shown as small coloured circles at the spatial reference of the Survey event, Sample or observation. Selecting 'Default' will display a distribution map of this form.

A key appears on the left-hand side of the Map browser window, which shows the symbols currently in use.

You can also switch the display of symbol sets on and off, edit the legends and choose the colour of symbols - see Map Options - Distribution Symbols.

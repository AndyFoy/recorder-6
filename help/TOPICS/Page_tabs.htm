Page tabs

Recorder can often record more details about an item than will fit onto a single screen. The details have been grouped into a number of pages and these are presented on the screen as a series of 'tabs'. The most frequently used details are grouped under the 'General' tab, which is the one that will be displayed when a window opens.

To display a particular page

  • Click on the appropriate label in the tab control with your mouse

OR

  • Press the <Tab> key to move focus around the screen until it arrives at the tab control.

  • A dotted rectangle will be shown around the label of the active tab.

  • Then use the right and left arrow keys to select the page you require.

Authors and Editors

The Documents window includes boxes containing a list of the names of authors and editors of documents. These are added, edited and deleted in the same way, except that the labels – Author(s) or Editor(s) – change as appropriate.

To add an author's name:

  • Choose the button.

  • The following dialog appears:

  • Enter at least the surname of the author, and preferably the initial as well. Both are free text fields.

  • Choose [OK], and the name will be added to the list.

To edit the name of an author:

  • Select one of the names in the list by clicking on it with your mouse, or positioning the highlight bar using the <up arrow>, <down arrow>, <PgUp>, <PgDn>, <Home> and <End> keys.

  • Choose the button.

  • The dialog appears with the surname and initials fields filled in with the selected name. Edit these two fields as necessary.

    • Choose [OK] to make the changes to the list.

    • Choose [Cancel] to abandon any changes.

To delete the name of an author:

  • Select one of the names in the list by clicking on it with your mouse, or positioning the highlight bar using the <up arrow>, <down arrow>, <PgUp>, <PgDn>, <Home> and <End> keys.

  • Choose the button.

  • You will be asked to confirm that you really want to delete the selected name.

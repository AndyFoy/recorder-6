Location Hierarchies

A location in Recorder uses the concept of sites and sub-sites. A site is the main location entity, and can be divided into any number of smaller entities or sub-sites. These sub-sites can form a hierarchy with any number of levels, and can be fairly complex. Information can be associated with sites and sub-sites in exactly the same way – using the detail panel on the right hand side of the location explorer.

The position of particular sites in the hierarchy can be changed by using either drag and drop or cut and paste in the Location tree view. To move a location in this way;

  • You must "own" the location, i.e. It must have been created on your copy of Recorder .

  • The details of the location to be moved must not conflict with those of its new parent, i.e. if the new parent has been associated with specific grid squares, the child must be within those grid squares.

Note: multiple locations can be selected for moving to a new position using <Shift> + left click of the mouse button or <Ctrl> + left click, as in Windows Explorer. They can then be dragged to the new position or moved to it by right clicking and selecting Promote to Top-Level or Move to. If Move to is used, the Find location dialogue box opens so that you can find the location to move them to.

 To move any site or sub-site to a different level using drag and drop

  • Click and hold down the left mouse button on the site or sub-site that you want to move.

  • The cursor changes to a drag cursor.

  • While holding down the button, drag the item to the site or sub-site that you want to move it to.

  • Release the mouse button.

  • You will be prompted to confirm you really want to make the change.

  • The dragged item becomes a sub-site of the designated site or sub-site.

To move any site or sub-site to a different level using cut and paste

You may prefer to use this method if you cannot see the original and destination positions of the site at the same time in the hierarchy.

  • Click on the site or sub-site that you want to move with the right mouse button.

  • Select Edit - Cut from the main menu.

  • Browse to the new position.

  • Right click on the new parent and select Edit - Paste from the main menu.

  • You will be prompted to confirm you really want to make the change.

  • The dragged item becomes a sub-site of the designated site or sub-site.

Note: Don't worry if you find that you cannot paste the site where you want to. Simply make the appropriate change and try again. You won't lose the site you are trying to move. If the 'paste' part of the operation is not completed, the 'cut' will also not occur.

To promote a sub-site to a site

  • Click on the sub-site that you want to promote with the right mouse button.

  • Click on the Promote to Top-Level menu item.

  • The sub-site is promoted to a site, and is displayed at the bottom of the tree view.

TIP: If you are moving or promoting a sub-site which already has a hierarchy beneath it, the hierarchy beneath it will be moved with the sub-site.

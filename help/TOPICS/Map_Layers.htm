Turning map layers on and off

Turning off a layer merely makes it invisible until such a time as you turn it on again. By turning off a layer you will not lose any of your data. Map layers can be turned on or off in two ways:

either

  • Open the map that you are interested in

  • Click on the checkbox in the first column of the Map Layers list in the Map window

  •  If a tick appears in the box, the map layer will be displayed.

or

  • Select Map - Map Options - Map layers page,

  • Ensure that the map you are interested in is selected from the drop-down list

  • Click next to the layers to turn them on or off

  •  If a tick appears in the box, the map layer will be displayed.

Background map layers will only appear when the scale of the current display is between its cut-in and cut-out scales.

Note: If you have turned on a layer and still can't see it, it may be due to the map layer order.

Survey Events Ownership Tab

The Survey Events Ownership tab lists the 'owner' of a survey event. This tab allows you to add or delete an owner for a survey event. An owner can be an individual or organisation other than the person/organisation running the survey, or the recorders of the observations. To add or delete an owner you must be in Edit mode.

Display Name

The name of an individual or organisation from the lists of Names and Addresses.

Type

The type of owner. Select one of the values from the drop-down box. The contents of this box is taken from the Survey Event Owner Type Term List. To add, edit or delete terms select Tools - Term Lists from the main menu.

To add an owner:

  • Use one of the standard methods for finding things to select the required owner from the lists of Names and Addresses.

  • When you have added the required owner and completed any changes on the other tabs, choose [Save] in the right-hand panel of the window to update the database with the changes for the selected survey event.

  • You can choose [Cancel] in the right-hand panel to abandon any changes. No changes will be made to the database, and the window will be restored to its original state.

To delete an owner:

  • Ensure that the owner you wish to remove is selected.

  • Click the Delete button on your keyboard.

  • Select the corresponding type.

  • Click the Delete button on your keyboard.

  • Click [Save] or [Cancel], as for adding an owner.

To edit an owner:

  • Remove it and add the required owner.

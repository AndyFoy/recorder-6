Data Entry

 

Enter Species for a place

Select a record entry card so that a list of taxa and/or a biotope from a sample can be entered rapidly.

Observations

View or manage observations, including surveys, survey events, samples, taxon and biotope occurrences.

Locations

View or manage records of locations.

Names and Addresses

View or manage names and addresses of individuals and organisations.

Documents

View or manage references to documents, both published and unpublished.

Enter a species record

Enter an individual taxon and/or biotope occurrence. This can be used to rapidly enter a series of related observations, for example, from the same documentary source or for the same species.

Display Unchecked Records

This finds observations which are not flagged as "Checked", or are flagged as "Zero abundance" or "Failed/pending verification". The Observation window will open with a filter applied so that only observations of the required type are displayed.

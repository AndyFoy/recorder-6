UTM Co-ordinates

Universal Transverse Mercator co-ordinates are specified by stating the sector number, followed by two real numbers in kilometers. The sector number is followed by one or more spaces, and the co-ordinates are separated by one or more spaces. The British Isles falls within sector 30. In some Recorder systems UTM co-ordinates have been used for Channel Isles data.

Examples:

30 525900 5478700 - WV259787 in the Channel Isles

30 559600 5447600 - WV596476 in the Channel Isles

Military style UTM grid refs

JNCC have written an addin which allows you to put in the Military style UTM grid refs like "WA143576".

Example:

"30U: WA143576"

where 30U is the sector for southern Britain

Please contact JNCC for information about this add-in if you require it

Mapping Un-Zoom

To zoom out:

  • Select the un-zoom tool:

    • Select Map – UnZoom from the Main menu.

    • Click the button on the active toolbar.

    • Press the short-cut key <F3>.

  • The map cursor will change to .

    • Click on the map, and the size of the map will be halved. It will be redrawn centred on the point at which you clicked.

    • Alternatively, click and hold down the primary mouse button and then drag the mouse. This will draw a rectangle on the map. When you release the mouse button, the map will be redrawn so that the whole screen is fitted within the rectangle you drew.

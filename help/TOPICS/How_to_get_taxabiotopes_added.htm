How to add taxa or biotopes to Dictionaries

The Natural History Museum manage the Species Dictionary project. If you have any comments or queries about the taxonomy within the taxon dictionary in Recorder or feel that an addition or change is needed, please contact:

Species Dictionary Project Manager
UK Biodiversity Programme
Department of Botany
The Natural History Museum
Cromwell Road
London SW7 5BD
United Kingdom

Tel. +44 (0)207 942 5213

E-mail: speciesdictioanry@nhm.ac.uk
Web site: www.nhm.ac.uk/nbn/

Requests for corrections and changes to the system supplied information in the Biotope Dictionary should be sent to JNCC (e-mail: Recorder@JNCC.gov.uk).

When changes become available you will be able to incorporate them into your system by running a dictionary upgrade.

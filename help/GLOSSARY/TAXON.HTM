Taxon

A taxon (plural: taxa) is a generic term for any category used in the classification of organisms, whatever its rank. The fundamental taxon is the species, and all other taxa are groupings or sub-divisions of species.

The term 'taxon' is more appropriate than 'species' for biological recording because observations are frequently recorded for taxa other than species (e.g. for sub-species or for families). Recorder allows a taxon occurrence to be recorded for any taxon in the Taxon dictionary.

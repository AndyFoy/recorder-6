WinZip

A widely used piece of software that is used to compress computer files into smaller sizes to:

  • reduce the space required to store the files on a computer (especially for backup or archiving purposes)

  • reduce the size of files to be transferred between computers so that large files can be fitted on to removable disks or to reduce the transmission times (and hence the cost) of email attachments or web downloads.

The resulting, compressed files are often referred to as "zip files" and the process as "zipping".

An evaluation versions of WinZip is often included on the cover-disks distributes with computer magazines and can be downloaded from www.winzip.com.

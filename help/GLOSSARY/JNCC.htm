JNCC (Joint Nature Conservation Committee)

JNCC is the statutory adviser to Government on UK and international nature conservation. Its work contributes to maintaining and enriching biological diversity, conserving geological features and sustaining natural systems. JNCC delivers the UK and international responsibilities of the four country nature conservation agencies - Council for Nature Conservation and the Countryside (Northern Ireland), the Countryside Council for Wales, Natural England and Scottish Natural Heritage.

JNCC commissioned the development of Recorder and they take the lead role in supporting it. For more information, see the Recorder web site, www.jncc.gov.uk/recorder.

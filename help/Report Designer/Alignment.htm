Alignment

These buttons control the alignment of the selected elements.

Only two buttons are active when a single element is selected, they will center the selected item in the band either horizontally or vertically .

If two or more elements are selected (hold down the [Shift] key whilst clicking on a series of elements to select more than one), then the alignment buttons will be active. These align the second and subsequent element you selected with the left-hand edge , right edge , top edge , bottom edge , horizontal center or vertical center of the first element you selected.

Finally, if three or more elements are selected, then the spacing buttons will be active. These space the selected elements equally, either horizontally or vertically between the outer two elements.

Adding a group band

  • Click on the "add new group band" button and then click on the layout area. The "Group band" dialog box will appear:

  • We need to identify the field which will control the group band - the band will be printed when the value of this field changes. Click on the button at the end of the "Group by field or expression".

  • Click the [Database field] button.

  • Select the field - in this case "Sample Location Name". Click [OK}

  • Click [OK] on the Expression Wizard dialog.

  • Click [OK] on the "BAND 4" dialog.

  • You should now see an empty band entitled "Group header" on your page. You now need to add the fields you want to appear on this band. Add "Sample Location Name" data field to this band by dragging it from the Data Fields list and set its position, size, font, etc.

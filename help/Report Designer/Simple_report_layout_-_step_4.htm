Simple report layout - step 4

Adding text to the title:

  • Click on the "add new label" button and then in the Title band. The following dialog will appear:

  • Type your title in the edit box and set the font you want for the title. You will probably want it larger and emboldened. You may also want to change the alignment to "Centered". Once you are happy, click [OK].

Tip: You can change your text at any time. Just double-click on the element and this dialog will re-open allowing you to edit the text or change its characteristics.

  • Use the alignment tools to help position the title element. For example, clicking the center vertically and horizontally buttons will position your title nicely in the middle of the band.

Grab handles

When you select a Report Element by clicking on it with the selection tool, it will be shown with "grab handles", 8 small black boxes at the corners and middles of the sides of the element.

You can drag these handles with your mouse to resize the object. Positions your mouse over one of the handles and it changes to a double headed arrow. Drag the handle and the size of the box will change:

  • position over the handle in the middle of the top or bottom side (cursor changes to a vertical arrow ) and drag to make the element wider or narrower.

  • position over the handle in the middle of the left or right side (cursor changes to a horizontal arrow ) and drag to make the element longer or shorter.

  • position over one of the handles at the corners (cursor changes to a sloped arrow ) and drag make the element larger or smaller in both width and length at the same time.

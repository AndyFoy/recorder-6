To add a second group band, linked to the first:

  • Add another group band by clicking the "add new group band" button and then click on the layout area. Select the controlling field (SAMPLE_VAGUE_DATE_START) as before. But this time, when you get back to the "BAND" dialog, select the existing group band (BAND 4 in this example) from the list in the "Link with band" prompt.
